Important to Note about Bonds

    Nominal Yield:

  • The annual interest rate is a % of the face value of a bond.
  • Principal is returned on maturity date, loan ends.

    • Expensive:

  • Retail investors can purchase bond indexes

    • Predetermined Intervals:

  • Company pays investors interest coupons on predetermined intervals.
  • For example, monthly, annually, and semiannually.
